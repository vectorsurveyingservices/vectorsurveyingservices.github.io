The Origin of Vector Surveying

Vector Surveying Services was founded in 2015 with a simple plan: only take surveying jobs that can be performed in an exacting manner and to do those jobs quickly and cost effectively. We realize that members of the land surveying industry rely on their reputations. Clients may not have the knowledge to judge the quality of the service being provided to them so they depend upon the testimony of other customers. Check out some of our testimonials - We strive to do exceptional work and treat everybody squarely.

Tim Hozalski, P.S.

Tim Hozalski, the owner of Vector Surveying Services, received a Bachelor's Degree in Surveying and Mapping from The University of Akron in 2010. Five years later Tim became a licensed Ohio Professional Surveyor. He takes great pride in his work and is passionate about all things he does. When he's not surveying, Tim also operates a crop farm with his brother and dad in Henrietta Twp, Ohio. On the farm, he specializes in metal fabrication. In the winter you will find him riding his snowmobile.
