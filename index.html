Professional Surveyors are the stewards of the nation's property boundaries and Vector Surveying Services LLC is Northern Ohio's premier professional land surveying firm. Serving Lorain, Erie, Huron, & Surrounding Counties

Vector Surveying Services, LLC is proud to offer land surveying services in Lorain, Erie, Huron and surrounding Northern Ohio counties. Our professional surveyors are licensed by the state of Ohio to perform boundary retracements, lot splits, ALTA surveys, consolidation surveys, elevation certificates, construction staking, and a number of other property mapping services.
